You found yourself awakening by the tranquil lakeside,
your head throbbing and your senses in disarray.
The very essence of your identity is a shrouded enigma,
lost in the mist of forgotten memories. However, an inexplicable
vigor pulses within you, compelling you towards the majestic castle
that graces the lake's opposite shore.
In the midst of uncertainty, you discover a compelling energy that
beckons you towards the castle's towering spires,
adorned with ancient mysteries and untold tales.
This journey is not only to unravel your own forgotten past
but to etch new legends into the annals of time.
With resolute determination, you embark upon this epic quest,
where your story is yet to be written.
